About Me

With previous experience as an electrical engineer, I have a passion for computer programming and a relentless drive for continuous improvement in all of my endeavors. After coding in undergrad and professionally as an engineer (not to mention as a hobby for decades) in the summer of 2021 I finally decided to become serious about software engineering, and took a 24-week immersive course at App Academy to jumpstart this pivot in my career. I have experience using Python, JavaScript, SQL, Node.js, ReactJS, and I've just started diving into lambda functions and S3 storage on AWS. I also have quite a bit of older experience coding in C++.

Something that I love about programming is the opportunity to work with people from a wide range of disciplines. The software development lifecycle involves working with so many different people and departments and, as a people person and someone who loves to build new relationships, it’s great to be able to come together with other professionals who are striving towards the same goal.

If you want to talk about programming, coding, software engineering, or anything else, drop me a line. I am currently available for employment.
